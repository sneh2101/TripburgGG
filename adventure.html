Thailand


Tour Type : Tours for the Specially abled    Tour Code #SATT

Your Itinerary

DAY 1
Bangkok – Pattaya (2 Nights)

Mumbai to Mumbai Tour’ Guests will meet at Chatrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai as per the scheduled flight departure time and proceed to Bangkok. Welcome to Bangkok and proceed to Pattaya. We visit the tallest building of Pattaya - Pattaya Tower and experience Speed Shuttle/ Sky Shuttle/ Tower Jump which gives breath taking view of the city. Later we enjoy the famous cabaret show - Alcazar Show in the evening..

Dinner

Night stay in Pattaya

DAY 2
Pattaya

Today we visit Nong Nooch village – biggest and most beautiful botanical garden & enjoy Pottery Garden, famous Elephant show and Thai cultural show. In the evening we relax ourselves by the Scientific Thai massage.

Breakfast        Lunch         Dinner

Night Stay in Pattaya

DAY 3
Pattaya – Bangkok (3 Nights)

Today we leave for Bangkok. En route, we visit Mini Siam – A miniature park, and famous and world’s biggest Gems Gallery where we enjoy shopping of precious stones and witness a spectacular show in light and sound presentation of the glittering world of gems. Later we proceed to Bangkok.

Breakfast        Lunch         Dinner

Night Stay in Bangkok

Highlights
World's biggest jewellery store - Gems Gallery
Lovely botanical gardens of Nong Nooch village
Enjoy elephant show and Thai cultural show

DAY 4
Bangkok

Today we visit the Safari World – an open zoo where visitors can marvel at the animals living in their natural habitat. At Marine Park, there are a variety of exotic marine animals and shows like Orang Utang Monkey Kick Boxing Show, Cowboy Stunt Show, Sea Lion Show, and Dolphin Show. Here we get an opportunity to have the closest encounter with rare and endangered species of the animal kingdom...



Breakfast       Lunch       Dinner

Bangkok
