Halong - Hanoi

Visit Sao Do handicraft center. Visit the world famous temples Angkor Thom -Southern gate, Elephant terrace and Lepper king terraces and ancient bridge, Bayon complex etc. Visit Ta Prohm Temple - A battle between nature and architecture in the Cambodian jungle. Angkor Wat - The largest religious monument in the world. Enjoy Cambodian traditional massage.
