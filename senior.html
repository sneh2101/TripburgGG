Your Itinerary

DAY 1
Bhuj – Tent City (1 Night)

‘Joining and Leaving Tour’ Guests will meet directly at Bhuj Airport as per group scheduled flight timing. Today we visit Vande Mataram Memorial at Bhuj. Later proceed to Tent City (Dhordo). On arrival we enjoy cultural activities at Tent City. In the evening we proceed to White Rann to witness the Grandeur of Sunset We have short meet and greet session before dinner.

Overnight stay at Tent City

DAY 2
Tent City (Dhordo) - Bhuj (1 Night) City Tour

Today we proceed to Bhuj. Today we visit Aina Mahal, Prag Mahal, Vijay Vilas palace and Mandvi beach.

Overnight stay at Bhuj

Breakfast        Lunch         Dinner

DAY 3
Bhuj – Muli (1 Night)

Today we leave for Muli. On arrival we visit Second oldest Swaminarayan Temple in the world and Wooden Haveli at Muli..

Overnight in Muli.

Breakfast        Lunch         Dinner

DAY 4
Muli – Modhera – Patan - Ahemdabad – Departure to hometown

Today early morning we go bird watching at Muli. Then we visit Modhera Sun Temple and Rani ki Vav at Patan. Later we leave for Ahmedabad. It’s time now to say goodbye to our tour companions. Let us be in contact with each other through email, Facebook…. And meet again on one more memorable tour. See you all!! ‘Joining and Leaving Tour’ Guests will be dropped at Ahmedabad airport. Tour concludes.
