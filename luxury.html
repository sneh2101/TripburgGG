Singapore Luxury Tour


Tour Type : Luxury Tours    Tour Code #LXSG

Your Itinerary

DAY 1
Destination – Singapore (3 Nights)

We begin our journey today! Guests will meet at Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai as per the scheduled flight departure time (Singapore Airlines Business Class) and fly to Singapore. On arrival, we check into Hotel Shangri-La or a luxury hotel of the same category. Time for some rest and later, we enjoy a delicious buffet at Shangri-La’s restaurant. The evening is set for a wonderful experience, as we visit Gardens by the Bay – Flower Dome & Cloud Forest. We enjoy a sumptuous dinner at the Rooftop super tree Restaurant of Gardens by the Bay that offers spectacular views of the city..

Overnight stay at Singapore

DAY 2
Singapore

It’s time to explore the Lion City! We drive through amazing sights such as Suntec City, Fountain of Wealth (largest fountain in the world), City Hall, Orchard Road, Parliament House, The Esplanade (performing arts centre), Marina Bay Sands and enjoy a photo stop at Merlion – an iconic landmark located at the Waterfront Merlion Park. We enjoy lunch at the Shahi Maharani Restaurant at Swissotel the Stamford (tallest hotel in Singapore). In the afternoon, we take a cable car ride from Mt. Faber to the fascinating world of Sentosa Island. Experience the thrill of Luge Ride with Sky Ride and visit attractions such as Images of Singapore and Madame Tussauds Wax Museum. The evening is set for a spectacular light and water display which combines lasers, fire effects and wonderful musical masterpieces to create a mesmerizing show – Wings of Time. Dinner will be something to look forward to as we dine on the beach at ‘Trappizza’ on Sentosa Island!

Overnight stay at Singapore

Breakfast        Lunch         Dinner

DAY 3
Singapore

A day dedicated to the wonder of Universal Studios! Experience unlimited entertainment, excitement, thrilling shows and attractions such as ‘Lights Camera Action’, Transformers, Accelerator ride, Revenge of the Mummy, Water World, Jurassic Park, Madagascar – A Crate Adventure, Donkey Live, Shrek 4D adventure, Flying Dragons etc. Lunch will be a memorable affair as we dine in the company of marine animals at Ocean Restaurant by Cat Core. We also visit Asia’s biggest aquarium here - S.E.A Aquarium which houses more than 100,000 marine animals and rare species of the marine world. In the evening, we head to the Marina Bay Sands observatory deck to enjoy a splendid night view of Singapore city. Dinner will be at the rooftop restaurant of Marina Bay Sands where we enjoy delicious food and admire stunning city views, at the same time..

Overnight in Singapore.

Breakfast        Lunch         Dinner

DAY 4
Singapore – Departure to home town

The final day brings the wonderful experience of shopping at Orchard Road. This will be followed by lunch at a fine dine restaurant on this iconic shopping street. It’s time now to say goodbye to this wonderful city and our travel companions. Let’s stay in touch through Email, Facebook, Mobile and meet each other again on more such memorable tours! This is not a goodbye but a ‘see you soon’. Guests will proceed to the airport as per their scheduled group flight time and take a return flight back to their home town.
Tour concludes.
