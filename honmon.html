Honeymoon Special Mauritius


Tour Type : Honeymoon Special    Tour Code #AFHM

Your Itinerary

DAY 1
Port Louis (6 Nights)

‘Mumbai to Mumbai Tour’ Guests will meet at Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai as per the scheduled flight departure time and proceed to Port Louis. Joining and leaving tour’ guests will meet the group as per their Convenience, directly at Port Louis Airport / Lunch restaurant/ Port Louis hotel according to the group schedule. Welcome to Port Louis – Capital of Mauritius..

Lunch      Dinner

Night stay in Port Louis

DAY 2
Port Louis

Today we visit Pamplemousses formally known as Sir Seewoosagur Botanic Gardens – it is home to an incredible variety of tropical plants, many of them are indigenous. We then have a panoramic view of Port Louis city from Citadel Fort. Later we explore the city orientation tour wherein we come across Catholic Church, Mosque, High Court building, Port Louis Theatre, French Colonial buildings etc. We shall enjoy shopping at Le Caudan waterfront in the evening..

Breakfast        Lunch         Dinner

Night Stay in Port Louis

DAY 3
Port Louis – Ile Aux Cerfs

Today we enjoy a high speed boat ride to Ile Aux Cerfs Island – the paradise island of Mauritius. On arrival, we will have a look of the beauty of the Island by taking a thrilling Para sailing ride. Later we enjoy swimming in the crystal clear waters of Mauritius. You have a chance to enjoy the aquatic activities like Banana Boat, Under Sea Walk and Jet scooter at additional cost. All aquatic activities are subject to weather conditions.

Breakfast        Lunch         Dinner

Night Stay in Port Louis

Highlights
High speed boat ride to Ile Aux Cerfs Island
Parasailing over Ile Aux Cerfs
Enjoy swimming in the crystal clear waters

DAY 4
Port Louis – Casela Bird Park

Today we visit Casela Bird Park – home to 150 species of birds from 5 different continents, including the lovely long necked Pink Pigeons which until recently were threatened with extinction. Later we shall enjoy shopping at Bagatelle mall of Mauritius....


Breakfast       Lunch       Dinner

Night Stay in Port Louis

Highlights
Explore the beautiful Island
Enjoy traditional Sega dance

DAY 5
Port Louis

Today we explore the beautiful island on our own. In the evening, we enjoy the Sega dance – A distinctive style of music and dance. Please Note: Hotels in Mauritius generally offer free activities such as boating, Kayaking and glass bottom ride. Guests can enjoy these activities in their free time.

Breakfast       Lunch       Dinner

Night Stay in Port Louis

DAY 6
Port Louis

Today we visit Chamarel village wherein we will come across Black River George, Seven coloured earths and Chamarel Waterfalls – best waterfalls in Mauritius. Later we visit Curious Corner- A world full of illusions, then we take a South Tour of Port Louis wherein we visit Trou Aux Cerfs also known as Murr’s Volcano – a dormant volcano but could become active at any time in next thousands years, Grand Bassin – the Sacred Lake and Shiva Temple and Model Ship Factory...
